# PantrySpark
Turn your pantry ingredients into recipe ideas &amp; smart shopping lists instantly.

What’s in your pantry? PantrySpark turns it into dinner.

Stop staring into your fridge wondering what to make. Just tell PantrySpark what ingredients you already have, and it instantly suggests up to 20 recipes you can cook right now — ranked by how well they match what’s on hand.

Pick your favorites, adjust servings to fit your needs, and PantrySpark builds a smart shopping list with ONLY the items you’re missing. No duplicates, no buying what you already have. Ingredients scale automatically when you change serving sizes.


🧊 MY PANTRY
Add the ingredients you have at home. PantrySpark remembers them and matches against our entire recipe library to find what you can cook right now.
✨ SMART RECIPE SUGGESTIONS
Get up to 20 recipe ideas ranked by ingredient match percentage. Each recipe card shows a real food photo, calorie count, protein, prep time, servings, and a nutrition grade (A/B/C/D). See exactly what you have vs. what you need.
📖 55+ FREE RECIPES WITH REAL PHOTOS
Browse breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snack recipes across cuisines — Italian, Asian, Mexican, Indian, Mediterranean, and more. Every recipe features beautiful food photography powered by Pexels. Filter by tags like high-protein, vegan, keto, quick, comfort food, and meal-prep.
🔍 DETAILED RECIPE CARDS
Tap any recipe to open a full detail view with hero photo, complete ingredient list, nutrition breakdown (calories, protein, carbs, fat), nutrition grade, and an adjustable serving size slider. Ingredients you already have are highlighted. Add to your meal plan with one tap.
🍽️ ADJUSTABLE SERVING SIZES
Change serving sizes on any recipe and all ingredient amounts automatically recalculate. Cooking for 2 instead of 4? One tap and every measurement scales perfectly. Your shopping list updates in real time too.
🛒 SMART SHOPPING LIST
Your shopping list only includes what you’re missing — pantry items are automatically excluded. Ingredients are grouped by category (Meat, Seafood, Dairy, Produce, Pantry), scaled to your chosen serving sizes, and include a satisfying progress tracker as you check items off.
➕ ADD YOUR OWN RECIPES
Upload your personal recipes with photos, ingredients (with amounts and units), nutrition info, prep time, servings, and custom tags. They integrate seamlessly into suggestions and shopping lists alongside built-in recipes.
📸 AI-POWERED FEATURES
Snap a photo of any food to estimate calories, protein, carbs, and fat using AI vision technology. Photograph a cookbook page, recipe card, or screen to automatically extract the recipe — name, ingredients, nutrition, and all — and add it to your collection instantly.
📊 NUTRITION TRACKER
Daily calorie donut chart showing progress toward your goal. Macro breakdown bars for protein, carbs, and fat. Weekly calorie bar chart to see your patterns. Streak tracker to stay motivated. Set custom daily nutrition goals and edit them anytime. All meals — both planned recipes and AI-scanned food — contribute to your daily totals.
📏 MEASUREMENT CONVERTER
Built-in kitchen measurement converter for when you don’t have the right tools. Convert between cups, tablespoons, teaspoons, ounces, grams, milliliters, Fahrenheit, Celsius, and more. Includes quick reference cards for common kitchen equivalents, an oven temperature chart, and practical tips like “a coffee mug is roughly 1 cup” for when you’re missing measuring tools.
🌙☀️ LIGHT & DARK THEMES
Beautiful dark mode with deep greens, blues, and purples with silver accents. Clean light mode for daytime use. Your theme preference is saved automatically. Switch anytime with one tap.
🔄 NEVER LOSE YOUR PLACE
Your current page is saved automatically. Refresh the app, close it, come back later — you’ll be right where you left off. No more starting over from the pantry screen.
